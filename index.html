Who We Are

We are a consortium of health ministers from all Ugandan universities and higher education institutions, uniting to create sustainable community health solutions.

Mission: To mobilize student-led health initiatives that address Uganda's most pressing health challenges through collaboration, innovation, and service.

Vision: A healthier Uganda where every community has access to quality health education and services, driven by the energy of university students.

Our Journey

The story of IUHMC from launch to today.

June 26, 2026
🚀 IUHMC Official Launch
Student leaders from across Uganda gathered at the Ministry of Health HQ to officially launch the Inter-University Health Ministers Consortium.

🩸 Sickle Cell Campaign Launched
The Sickle Cell Disease Campaign was launched with the theme "Creating a Window of Hope for Sickle Cell Among Young Adults."
July 10, 2026
📋 Central Region Meeting
First regional meeting of health ministers from Central Uganda universities to coordinate health activities.
August 15-16, 2026
📚 Northern Region Training
Lira University hosts the first Northern Region Sickle Cell Training for health ministers from all universities in the region.
